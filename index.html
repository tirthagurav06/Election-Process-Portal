Election Commission of India

The Election Commission of India (ECI) is a constitutional body under Article 324 responsible for conducting free and fair elections in India.

  • Conducts elections for Lok Sabha & State Assemblies
  • Maintains electoral rolls
  • Implements Model Code of Conduct
